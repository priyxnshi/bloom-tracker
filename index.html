🎯 Your Productivity Planner

Based on your cycle phase, here's how to optimise your time and energy.

🩸 Menstrual (Days 1–5)

Energy: Low — Rest mode

  • Catch-up on admin, emails, reading
  • Solo, low-stimulation work
  • Avoid big decisions or presentations
  • Journalling & reflection

🌱 Follicular (Days 6–13)

Energy: Rising — Creative mode

  • Start new projects, brainstorm
  • Learn new skills, take courses
  • Schedule important meetings
  • Collaborative and creative tasks

✨ Ovulation (Days 14–16)

Energy: Peak — Power mode

  • Presentations, pitches, interviews
  • Negotiations & big decisions
  • High-intensity workouts
  • Social events & networking

🌙 Luteal (Days 17–28)

Energy: Declining — Detail mode

  • Detail-oriented, analytical work
  • Editing, reviewing, organising
  • Gentle movement only
  • Prep for next menstrual rest phase
